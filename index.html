About Me

I am broadly interested in connecting new theoretical ideas for physics beyond the Standard Model to present and near-future experiments. My research has been primarily focused on neutrino physics, an exciting and promising research area that will remain at the focus of experimental and theoretical inquiry for some time to come.

Research

I am currently a member of the NOvA Collaboration, working on different aspects of the long-baseline neutrino oscillation experiment, NOvA, at Fermilab. I am particularly intereseted in BSM effects on neutrino oscillations like sterile neutrinos, non-standad interactions and neutrino decay. (A complete list of the NOvA publications can be seen here.)
  • Monte Carlo method for constructing confidence intervals with unconstrained and constrained nuisance parameters in the NOvA experiment, JINST 20 T02001 (2025)
  • Search for CP-Violating Neutrino Nonstandard Interactions with the NOvA Experiment, Phys. Rev. Lett. 133 201802 (2024)
  • Expanding neutrino oscillation parameter measurements in NOvA using a Bayesian approach, Phys. Rev. D110 012005 (2023)
  • Measurement of νμ charged-current inclusive π0 production in the NOvA near detector, Phys. Rev. D107 112008 (2023)

I am also a member of the DUNE Collaboration, risponsable of designing and building the next long-baseline neutrino oscillation experiment at Fermilab (see the list of related publications here).
  • DUNE Phase II: scientific opportunities, detector concepts, technological solutions, JINST 19, 12 P12005 (2024)
  • First measurement of the total inelastic cross section of positively charged kaons on argon at energies between 5.0 and 7.5 GeV, Phys. Rev. D110 092011 (2024)
  • Performance of a Modular Ton-Scale Pixel-Readout Liquid Argon Time Projection Chamber, Instruments 8, 3 41 (2024)

At Universidad del Atlantico, I've been working with Prof. Alexander Oliveros, implementing data analysis methodes to study interestig dark energy and/or modified-gravity models.
  • Cosmological dynamics and observational constraints on a viable f(Q) nonmetric gravity model, Int. J. Mod. Phys. D33 01, 2450004 (2024)
  • Late-time cosmology in a model of modified gravity with an exponential function of the curvature, Phys. Dark Univ. 40 101207 (2023)
  • Barrow holographic dark energy with Granda–Oliveros cutoff, Eur. Phys. J. Plus 137 7, 783 (2022)
